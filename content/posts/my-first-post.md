While the urge to stay in bed all day during the long winter holiday is a constant, and my senior-itis is at an all-time high, I decided to put together this little blog, if for no other reason than to justify the couple of cents my domain name costs me each month.

It certainly doesn't help that every job application in the computer science industry asks for a personal site, almost implying that a personal site is almost a necessity nowadays, and you're at a disadvantage for not having one, as opposed to just a nifty side project to show off.

I plan to post tutorials or articles on things I *think* I understand, perhaps in the hope that somewhere down the line some undergrad will use what I write to piece together their assignment.

But if not, well, at least it's another thing to add to my portfolio, and thats worth something. Right? *Right??*
