Over the last semester I had a few friends who were taking Ryerson's compulsory security course, CPS-633. As seems to be the case in a lot of undergraduate courses, it's about 10 miles wide, but really only a couple of inches deep. Nevertheless, it's an incredibly interesting course, and in itself covers a good majority of what I found when I took CompTIA's SY501 Security+ exam.

I found myself going over all the concepts based on notes I wrote a couple years ago, and figured it could be a good idea to host articles on various concepts. As such, you'll find a new tab, which will host all these random snippets of information that maybe, someone, someday, will find useful.
